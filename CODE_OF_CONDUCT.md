# Code of conduct

CDNFoundry contributors and maintainers must keep project spaces respectful,
professional, and free from harassment. Disagreement about technical choices is
welcome when it stays focused on evidence, safety, maintainability, and the
project contract.

Unacceptable behaviour includes personal attacks, discriminatory or sexualized
language, intimidation, deliberate disruption, publishing another person's
private information, or retaliation for a good-faith report.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when
necessary to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately through
the repository maintainer contact or GitHub's reporting tools. Reports will be
handled as confidentially as practical.
